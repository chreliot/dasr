Want to join the minor? The best first step is to make a short appointment with the minor’s advisor, Christopher Eliot (email), to discuss your interests, make sure you have a sense of the minor’s requirements, and answer any other questions you have. Dr. Eliot’s office is at 104F Heger Hall (see Heger on campus map).

If you are ready to fill out the forms to register, you can visit Joanne Herlihy, Senior Assistant in the Philosophy Department. Her desk is at 104 Heger Hall. She is normally available during business hours, but you can also call ahead, phone (516) 463-5612.
